How to use Hebrew Verbs 🐱

🃏 Flashcards

Flip the card, then be honest with yourself:

  • ✓ Got it — you knew the answer before flipping. Builds mastery.
  • ✗ Again — you hesitated or got it wrong. The card returns soon.

⌨️ Type It

Type the full translation. Spelling counts. Wrong answers come back automatically later in the session.

❓ Quiz

12 multiple-choice questions mixing Hebrew ↔ English.

נִקּוּד Nikud toggle

Shows vowels on Hebrew text. Study with it ON first, then switch it OFF to train real reading. Mastery is only counted without nikud.

📋 Review Deck

After 3 misses in a row, a verb moves here automatically. Each time you study the Review Deck, it alternates between Flashcards and Type It.

⭐ Mastery levels

Select a verb from the dropdown on the home screen to check your level: Beginner → Familiar → Good → Mastered.

🔊 Audio pronunciation

Tap the ℹ button on any card to open the verb panel, then tap 🔊 Listen or 🔊 Hear sentence.

Hebrew audio uses your device's built-in voice:

  • iPhone / Mac — works out of the box (voice: Carmit)
  • Android / Chrome — works out of the box (Google Hebrew)
  • Windows — go to Settings → Time & Language → Language → Add a language → עברית, then restart the browser

If the first tap produces silence, tap once anywhere on the page and try again.
